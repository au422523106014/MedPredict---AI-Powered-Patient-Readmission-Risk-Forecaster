🏥 MedPredict – AI-Powered Patient Readmission Risk Forecaster

📌 Project Overview

MedPredict is a Machine Learning-based healthcare application developed to predict the risk of patient readmission using Electronic Health Records (EHR). By analyzing patient information such as age, medical history, diagnosis, comorbidities, and length of hospital stay, the system helps identify patients who may require additional medical attention after discharge.

The project demonstrates the application of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in healthcare to support better clinical decision-making and improve patient care.

---

🎯 Objective

The objective of this project is to develop an AI-powered prediction system capable of identifying patients with a higher probability of hospital readmission, enabling healthcare providers to take preventive measures and optimize treatment planning.

📊 Dataset

The dataset contains patient-related healthcare information including:

- Patient Age
- Gender
- Diagnosis
- Medical History
- Comorbidities
- Length of Hospital Stay
- Readmission Status

The dataset is used for training and testing the machine learning model.
